About Us

Our Story

Novioplast Enterprise was founded in 2022 with a mission to provide dependable, compassionate, and high-quality support services across a range of industries.

The business began as a small care-focused service, driven by a passion for improving the lives of elderly individuals and vulnerable people in the community. Over time, demand for our trustworthy and hardworking team grew, and we expanded our services to include support work, residential and commercial cleaning, hospitality staffing, and general labor such as heavy lifting and relocation support.

What sets Novioplast Enterprise apart is our commitment to integrity, attention to detail, and personalized care—whether we're helping someone get through their daily routine, ensuring a clean and welcoming environment, or supporting clients through physically demanding tasks.
